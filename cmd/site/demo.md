## arithmetic

the quadratic formula solves any equation of the form
$ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ and is given by:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

## fractions and nesting

euler's identity: $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$

a nested fraction: $\frac{\frac{a}{b}}{\frac{c}{d}}$

a continued fraction fragment: $1 + \frac{1}{1 + \frac{1}{1 + \frac{1}{x}}}$
